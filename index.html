Mindscribe is packed with features designed to enhance your learning experience and make note-taking more effective than ever.

Rich Media Support

Enhance your notes with images, links, and embedded content. Our AI can even analyze images to provide insights and descriptions.

Smart Organization

Tag, search, and filter your notes with ease. Our intuitive interface helps you find exactly what you need when you need it.

Dark & Light Themes

Switch between beautiful dark and light themes to match your environment and preferences. Your eyes will thank you.

Export & Share

Export your notes in multiple formats including TXT, PDF, and HTML. Share your knowledge with others effortlessly.

Privacy First

How Mindscribe Works

Getting started with Mindscribe is simple and intuitive. Here's how you can transform your learning process in just a few steps.

1

Create Your First Note

Start by creating a new note. Give it a title and begin writing your thoughts, ideas, or study materials.

2

Enhance with AI

Use the AI assistant to summarize content, generate action items, brainstorm ideas, or get explanations for complex topics.

3

Organize & Tag

Add tags to categorize your notes. Use the search functionality to quickly find what you're looking for.

4

Review & Export

Review your notes in a clean, distraction-free interface. Export them to share with others or for offline access.
